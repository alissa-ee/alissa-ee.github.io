GIANT TARO

Alocasia macrorrhiza


Alocasia macrorrhizos, commonly called giant taro or elephant ears, is native to Southeast Asia and is treasured both for its giant decorative leaves which resemble the ears of an elephant and its edible rhizomes which have been cultivated for many years in the tropics as a vegetable under the name of taro.


    Easy to intermediate care


    Medium to bright, indirect light


    Toxic to pets if consumed
